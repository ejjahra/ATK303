PUG LOAF: BREAD FOR GREATNESS

  • STRENGTH: He can disguise himself as a loaf of bread.
  • WEAKNESS: He looks like a loaf of bread.
  • ENEMY: Humans who think he’s actually a loaf of bread.
  • HOW HE FIGHTS: He doesn’t. He just rolls around.
  • GOAL: Avoid the enemy. Stay hidden.

GAME MECHANICS:

  1. Roll around and avoid hungry humans!
  2. Stealth missions: Sneak into the kitchen to steal some food.

DESIGN PROCESS:

My brain always goes to pugs. I wasn’t intending for it to look like a loaf of bread, but it did.

Then I remembered the meme of a pug that looks like bread, so, happy accident.
